Amruta Yelamanchili

I'm a software engineer in the Artificial Intelligence Group at the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory.

As a member of the AI group, I developed and deployed scheduling systems in use for the operations of the Mars 2020, ECOSTRESS, and OCO-3 missions.

I graduated from UC Berkeley in 2017 with degrees in Computer Science and Applied Math.

At Berkeley, I was a TA for CS10 - The Beauty and Joy of Computing, CS61B - Data Structures and Algorithms, CS186 - Database Systems, and CS375 - Teaching Techniques for Computer Science.

I formerly volunteered at the Marine Mammal Care Center in Los Angeles, taking care of sick, injured, and malnourished seals and sea lions.
